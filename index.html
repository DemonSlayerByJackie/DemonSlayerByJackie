Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko Kamado is from the anime Demon Slayer and is also in the two demons slayer movies, Mugen Train.

Qualitys:Daring, Strong, Responsible

Birthdate: December 28

She is a demon and the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the two remaining members of the Kamado family. Formerly a human, she was attacked and turned into a demon by Muzan Kibutsuji.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma is from the anime Demon Slayer and is friends with Tanjiro and Insoke and travels with them on jorneys.

Qualitys:Strong, Fast, Brave

Birthdate: Zenitsu Agatsuma was born on September 3rd.

The user of Thunder Breathing and one of the main characters from the series, Zenitsu Agatsuma was born on September 3rd. As clumsy as he looks, when it’s showtime, you can count on this guy. Zenitsu has two sides to him. One is the normal, usual self. While the other one takes over when there’s danger nearby
